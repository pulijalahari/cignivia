ABOUT CIGNIVIA

WHO WE ARE

Cignivia is a national mid-sized consulting firm positioned between the small boutiques and the big 6 – big enough for great opportunities and small enough for your accomplishments to be noticed and recognized. We specialize in delivering superior quality consulting and staffing solutions to our client partners, enabling them to compete in today’s competitive and dynamic business environment. We have over 10 years of experience in staffing consultants in Java, .Net, QA, Project Management, ERP, BA, EA in Federal, Healthcare, Retail and Financial domains. Cignivia is an SBA 8(a) certified company.


OUR TEAM

Padma Pulijala, CEO

Padma Pulijala is the CEO of Cignivia, a NextGen Digital Technology Solutions and consulting company based out of Atlanta, GA. Padma's vision and leadership led Teknaux to be a robust staffing organization in the region. Padma also plays a very important role in transforming Cignivia into a digital services and solution provider with a goal to deliver Quality, Value and Change to every client, every time.

She is a mentor, a people-first leader and has a knack for simplifying the most complex problems. Separate from her passion for deep technology, Padma Pulijala is a strong advocate for social upliftment through “Education for Everyone” and women empowerment. She has helped fund and direct many NGO's in the Asian Sub Continent that work for the upliftment and protection of women.
